1. How can you track what challenges the player has solved?
Create booleans and make them true when the player completes a task.
2. How can you track HP?
Set a base health for the player, when the player loses health it will subtract it from the total. If you had health regen, it will add health until you are full HP.
3. Items?
Start the player off with no items, when the collect items, it will add it to their inventory.
